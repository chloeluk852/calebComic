Bacon


Bacon does not appear much in the comics, but appears more in other comics. He used to be an antagonist, but then became good and joined the nostalgia gang. He used to use Water stream rock smashing fist, but then he found dangerous cosmic powers and became a cosmic terror. Right now, he kept his cosmic powers, but he rarely uses them.

PERSONALITY:Bacon is a fearless character, who always desires to get stronger. He got his wish when he discovered the cosmic powers, and when he was a cosmic terror, he became more rude and had a tendency to warp people onto the moon.

APPEARANCE: Bacon is a bacon hair with a black jacket, a blue shirt, sometimes a yellow martial arts sash, and dark green pants with white-black sneakers.
