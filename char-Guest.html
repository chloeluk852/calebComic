Guest


Guest is a protagonist in the comics. He is a cyborg. He uses his metal arms to fight. He also has many ranged attacks, such as shooting blasts of energy at enemies, or shooting laser beams from his hands. He is noob’s sidekick.

PERSONALITY:Guest has a cold personality, not really caring about much. He is always serious, and seldom smiles. He talks a lot in his explanations, so much that he could go on for hours if no one stops him.

APPEARANCE: Guest wears a baseball cap that covers his hair, with a “R” on it. he wears a black shirt and black pants, and his shirt has the word “Roblox” on it. He is 170 cm tall.
