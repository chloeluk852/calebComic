Green


Green is the secondary protagonist of the Nostalgia Gang comics. He is the host of Bot, a power that allows you to use raw destructive power in combat, and gains the user access to a powerful state called the 100% Bot form. In this state, Green uses destructive power in his attacks and the attacks cause lots of damage, and are very powerful. His way of killing foes are in one quick, powerful blow, and that usually ends fights really quickly.

PERSONALITY: Green is a helpful person, and does not really like to harm people unless he has to. He is also really brave in a fight and does not run away from a confrontation.

APPEARANCE: Green wears a black jacket, a black scarf, a coloured shirt, a red Roblox visor on his head, has brown charmer hair, and wears black pants and boots. His height is 170 Cm. Sometimes he wears a suit.
