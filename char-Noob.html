Noob


Noob is the Main Protagonist of the Nostalgia gang comics. He has the powers of one punch man.

PERSONALITY: Noob is a strong willed, kind, but sadly not very bright person. he usually is bored, due to the fact of nobody being able to defeat him in a fight. If he is pushed too far, he will go into a rage mode and attack with a serious face on.

APPEARANCE: Noob has yellow skin, is bald, and wears a blue shirt and lime green pants. he has a simple smiley face, and a blocky body. He is 168 cm tall.

After Noob dies while using saitama’s powers, he will recieve Last breath San’s powers through a binding vow, and if he gets killed in all the last breath forms, he will use Xiao Ling’s TIX eyes power.
