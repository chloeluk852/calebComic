Xiao Ling


Xiao Ling is a protagonist in the comics. He is the strongest sorcerer in roblox, and is Green’s teacher of using sorcery. Xiao Ling often refers to himself in the third person. Xiao Ling is also Green’s brother. He uses the TIX eyes, and is known as the “Coil expert”.

PERSONALITY: Xiao Ling is a friendly character, helping the nostalgia gang as much as he can, and offering assistance in many battles.

APPEARANCE: Xiao Ling wears an outfit similar to Green’s except his hairstyle is different and he has darker brown hair than Green does. He is 171 cm tall.
