Pork


Pork is a supporting character in the nostalgia gang comics. he is a martial arts master of the water stream rock smashing fist. he taught bacon the style when he was younger.

PERSONALITY: Pork is a easily amused person, but having the most experience of all the gang members, because he is the oldest one.

APPEARANCE: Pork is a bacon hair, he wears a black and blue shirt, black pants, and white sneakers. he is 166 cm tall.
