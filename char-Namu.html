Namu


The Namu is a supporting antagonist in the comics. he is a dangerous creature that no one knows where it came from. It is powerful and destructive. Upon defeating him you will get more chances of becoming a portal master.

PERSONALITY: Namu has the intelligence of a animal, and behaves like one too. He only roars or growls when he is fighting, and is brutal and devastating in his attacks, and despite lacking intelligence, Namu is shown to be sadistic, smiling after a kill. He is a C tier fighter, and a dragon level threat.

APPEARANCE: Namu wears nothing except for a pair of ripped trousers, he has a exposed brain, a birdlike head with sharp teeth, a muscular build, and claws on his limbs. He is 340 cm tall.
