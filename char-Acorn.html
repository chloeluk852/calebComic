Acorn


Acorn is another protagonist in the comics. she is a Psychic, and can use Telekinesis to use large objects in battle.

PERSONALITY: Acorn is a fiery person, never leaving a fight, and managing to intimidate villains enough for them to reveal secret villain plans. She is the most powerful psychic in all of robloxia.

APPEARANCE: Acorn has reddish-brown hair, wears a blue jacket, with a white striped shirt, pink pants, and black shoes. She is 162 cm tall.
