Jellytalking


Jellytalking is a tritagonist in the nostalgia gang comics. Jellytalking goes gambling to fuel his jackpot ultimate, and he is also a Kitsune buddha user, with 3 tails. He can combine all three of his powers to create his strongest form. He also has the powers of W.D Gaster.

PERSONALITY: Jelly is a very brash person, he likes to attack first and ask questions later. He does not think much before acting as he lost a lot of brain cells hitting his head with his anti plot armor metal bat.

APPEARANCE: Jellytalking is a bacon hair, he wears a black and blue shirt, sometimes a Gaster jacket, black pants, and white sneakers. he is 167 cm tall. He also can manifest glowing gaster hands around him.
